Features

What will you get from GroceryAssistant

Google Maps Integration

Google Maps integration helps you to look which grocery stores are the closest to you. Also, you can easily open the store you select with the Google Maps app and it will give you the direction to the store!

Store Information

We provide with accurate information for the nearby stores with the help of Google Cloud's Places API.
The information includes store's address, website, telephone number, rating, and opening hours.

Personal Grocery List

We provide you with a personal grocery list which helps you to plan your grocery and stay focused on what you really need.
You can easily search and add any items to your list. Also, sorting your list by name, price, and recently added.

Department Look-up

You can look for any items by their department category. For example, deli, dairy, frozen, meat, and produce.

Grocery Item Information

1. You can check the price and also if an item is on sale.
2. Make healthy choices by checking the nutrition facts.
3. Easily know where the items are located in the store.

Recipes

There is a lot of recipes for you to make! We give you the list of ingredients and step-by-step instruction to make your dish of choice.
